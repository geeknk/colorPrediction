Welcome to Color Prediction

Welcome to the Color Prediction Game, an exciting and interactive experience that tests your intuition and color recognition skills!

How to Play : In this game, players are presented with a series of colors and must predict the next color based on the patterns and trends displayed. The objective is simple: make accurate predictions to score points!

1. Choose Your Colors: Players can select from a vibrant palette of colors.
2. Make Your Prediction: Based on the displayed colors, predict which color will come next.
3. Score Points: Correct predictions earn points, while incorrect guesses may lead to a loss of points.
4. Track Your Progress: Monitor your score and improve your skills as you play!

Features
Vibrant Graphics: Enjoy a visually appealing interface with a wide range of colors.
Multiple Levels: The game offers various levels of difficulty, catering to both beginners and experienced players.
Daily Challenges: Engage with new challenges every day to keep the gameplay fresh and exciting.
Leaderboards: Compete with friends and players around the world to see who has the best color prediction skills.

Why Play?
The Color Prediction Game is not only fun but also sharpens your cognitive skills. It encourages quick thinking, pattern recognition, and decision-making—all while having a blast with colors!
Are you ready to dive into the world of colors and challenge your predictive abilities? Let’s see how well you can predict the next color! ---
